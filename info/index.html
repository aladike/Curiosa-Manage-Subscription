Research project

Regulatory monitoring for data protection and digital law

Curiosa is an automated research system for tracking and prioritising developments across GDPR, AI Act, and related frameworks. It collects updates from regulators, authorities, and legal sources daily — and delivers them as structured briefings for compliance and legal professionals.

How it works

Regulatory authorities

News titles and sources are always preserved as original — no rewriting, no paraphrasing. Only the summary is AI-generated: a concise, factual extract with minimal interpretation.

AI-assisted ranking

Each item is processed through a pipeline of 12 prompts that structure, classify, and summarise content with minimal creativity. Ranking combines AI priority signals with rule-based metrics across five principles: authority weight, signal type (Action / Watch / Monitor / FYI), source diversity cap, AI priority score with confidence, and tier partitioning into high and low relevance.

Source personalisation

Choose which sources to follow. Manage delivery settings at any time. Email addresses are stored in a database, never shared or sold, and protected at rest.
